What This Means for On Point

Tenco Solar, Stronghold Engineering, and Powerfull Solar collectively capture essentially all organic traffic in On Point's commercial-solar market. Critically, this isn’t a difficulty problem: the 172 gap keywords average a Keyword Difficulty of just 20, and 155 of them sit under KD 30 — very winnable terms. On Point simply has no page targeting any of them. Every month it stays off keywords like “commercial solar” (6,600/mo) and “commercial solar installers” (2,900/mo), businesses shopping for solar build their shortlists without On Point on them. (Note: Stronghold is a general engineering/GC firm; commercial solar is one division — but its solar pages still out-rank On Point on 109 of these terms.)

Pipeline Impact — The Revenue Math
The 50,900 monthly searches On Point doesn’t appear in carry an average CPC around $17 — that’s the market’s own valuation of this intent. Capturing even a conservative 1% of that volume as visits (~500/mo, Est.) and converting at a modest 2% to a consultation request yields roughly 10 qualified commercial-solar consults per month from a channel currently producing effectively none. At commercial-solar project values, even a single additional closed project a quarter represents six figures in revenue — and these are terms On Point can realistically rank for given their low difficulty.
